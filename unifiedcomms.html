Unified Communications

Help you interact seamlessly with partners, colleagues, and customers however far away.

  • Presence and instant messaging
  • Voice and video
  • Desktop sharing
  • Online meetings and conferencing
  • Application sharing
  • Virtual whiteboard

Ace IT Networks can deploy and implement Microsoft Lync Online to provide affordable enterprise class unified communications to businesses of any size. The solution is an integral part of Office 365 which provides a hosted solution eliminating the need for capital investment in on premises servers.

Lync Online connects people everywhere, on devices running Windows 8 and other operating systems, as part of their everyday productivity experience. Lync provides a consistent, single client experience for presence, instant messaging, voice, video and a great meeting experience. Lync enables instant messaging (IM) and voice calling with the hundreds of millions of people around the world who use Skype.
