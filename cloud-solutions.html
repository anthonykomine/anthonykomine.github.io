Cloud Solutions

 

Hosted server resources simplify on premises IT systems.

Ace IT Networks can deploy and implement a range of cloud based solutions to meet specific business needs. These solutions are typically charged on a monthly/annual subscription which can prove more flexible whilst avoiding the initial capital and ongoing maintenance costs of on premises servers.

These solutions include:

 Hosted Exchange
Microsoft Office 365
SharePoint
Online data backup
Hyper-V virtual server hosting
Cloud managed antivirus software

 

Cloud solutions are not always the best or most cost effective solution, and we are happy to assist with evaluating pros and cons of the options available to ensure you get the right balance of cost, performance and reliability for your business.

Microsoft Office 365 offers an very attractive cloud solution encompassing Exchange e-mail, SharePoint portal and Lync unified communications platform. For more details click here.
