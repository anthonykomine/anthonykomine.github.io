Remote Access – VPN

Remote access extends the benefit of your network resources to mobile and home workers whilst allowing data to be consolidated at a central location.

An effective and efficient solution is to connect to the corporate network across the Internet through a virtual private network (VPN). A VPN utilises sophisticated encryption and encapsulation technology to allow data to be securely transmitted across the Internet. Remote workers and branch offices can establish network connections from any location using their local Internet connection. This solution is becoming increasingly effective as the speed of broadband Internet connections steadily increase..

VPN forms an integral feature of many firewall systems. Academy can provide a range of VPN solutions using Windows Servers and most major brands of firewall device including:

 Watchguard
Cisco
Juniper NetScreen
SonicWALL
ZyXEL
Draytek

Academy use remote access as a means to monitor and support client systems which allows simple issues to be resolved quickly and cost effectively. We have considerable experience in implementing remote user connectivity and can provide an effective solution to make your network resources available to all your personnel.
